# Lunation

🌑New Moon: Novilunium (Soli coniuncta)  
🌒 Crescent: Cornicularis (in aspectu sextili)  
🌓 Quarter: Dimidiata (in aspectu quadrato)  
🌔 Gibbous: In orbem insinuata (in aspectu trino vel trigono)  
🌕 Full: Plenilunium (Soli opposita)
