Verse I
---
The year turns over like a body on concrete
Midnight hits and the city flinches
The mass screams at the sky like it owes them mercy
Glass shakes, beasts run, offspring wakes choking on smoke

Black snow falls into open mouths
Champagne mixes with blood on the street
Reason strikes the fuse, hands trembling
Charred flesh does not move

They call it joy
They call it tradition
A rite of fire, with reason gone

Pre Chorus
---
A rocket goes sideways
A balcony becomes a firing range
Hands disappear in a white flash
Phones record instead of helping

Sirens stack on top of each other
Like feedback that never ends
Fireworks bloom while ambulances crawl
Red and blue lights learn the same dance

Chorus I
---
Fire in the sky
Fire in the hands
Fire in the head
Fire in the plan

New year, old noise
Same stupid crown
We worship the explosion
And call it a countdown

Verse II
---
Count it down, count it wrong
Ten to zero, brain goes numb
Sing the future, scream the past
Hope burns fast, ashes last

In one street people cheer
In the next one people fight
Alcohol and pride shake hands
The calendar gives permission

A trash can burns like a small sun
Someone throws a bottle into a crowd
Riot police wait like a second wave
Everyone swears it was not their fault

Chorus II
---
Fire in the sky
Fire in the hands
Fire in the head
Fire in the plan

New year, same scars
Same names read aloud
We light the fuse together
Then deny the sound

Bridge
---
The sky looks beautiful for five seconds
Then it smells like sulfur and regret
The dead do not hear the countdown
The injured never forget

Same song every year
Same lie, different date
We blow things up to feel alive
Then act surprised by the weight

Chorus III
---
Fire in the sky
Fire in the hands
Fire in the head
Fire in the plan

New year, nothing new
Just smoke coming down
Another year learned nothing
While the city burns it down

Outro
---
When the smoke clears
Nothing is clean
The calendar flips
But the violence stays routine
